Calendar

Track recurring obligations and upcoming dates. As dates get closer, linked items become more visually important.

Calendar Pressure means a date gets visually louder as it gets closer.

Example: rent due on the 1st can make the linked Rent choice move closer to the center during the final week of the month.

This gives you a visual handle on what is coming at you soon, without needing to read a long list every day.

Calendar items are stored locally in this browser. They do not sync anywhere yet.
